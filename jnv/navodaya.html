🏫 PM Shri Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya - Naichana 🏫

About

PM Shri Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya, located in Naichana, is a premier educational institution providing quality education to rural students. Established under the Navodaya Vidyalaya Scheme, it offers free education from Class 6 to 12, focusing on academic excellence, holistic development, and national integration. The school is known for its modern facilities, dedicated faculty, and commitment to nurturing young minds.

Highlights

  • Academic Excellence: Quality education with focus on science, mathematics, and languages.
  • Modern Infrastructure: Well-equipped classrooms, laboratories, library, and sports facilities.
  • Residential Facility: Separate hostels for boys and girls with comfortable living conditions.
  • Holistic Development: Emphasis on sports, arts, cultural activities, and personality development.
