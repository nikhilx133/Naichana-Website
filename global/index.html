Global Education Center

About

The Global Education Center, Naichana was inaugurated on 17th August 2024 with the vision of providing holistic and modern education from Class 1 to 12. The Center is run by experienced educationists Shri Naresh Kumar and Mrs. Kiran Sharma for the benefit of students of Naichana.

Highlights

  • Inauguration: Established on 17th August 2024 — vision for holistic, modern education.
  • Academics: Classes 1–12 with a strong academic foundation, values, and discipline.
  • Technology & Growth: On-site Computer Education Center for digital skills and programming.
