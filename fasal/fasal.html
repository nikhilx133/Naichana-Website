🌾 Crops (Fasal) - Naichana 🌾

About

Agriculture is the backbone of Naichana's economy, with farmers cultivating various crops throughout the year. The fertile land and favorable climate support the growth of traditional crops like Millet (Bajra) and Dhaincha, which are essential for both local consumption and sustainable farming practices. These crops not only provide food security but also contribute to soil health and agricultural diversity in the region.

Highlights

  • Traditional Farming: Cultivation of age-old crops using sustainable agricultural practices.
  • Millet (Bajra): A drought-resistant crop that thrives in arid conditions and provides essential nutrition.
  • Dhaincha: A green manure crop that improves soil fertility and structure.
  • Agricultural Heritage: Preserving traditional farming knowledge and crop varieties for future generations.
