🏥 Common Health Centre & Medical Stores - Naichana 🏥

About

The Common Health Centre in Naichana serves as a vital healthcare facility providing essential medical services to the local community. Staffed with dedicated healthcare professionals including ANMs and doctors, it offers primary healthcare, maternal and child health services, immunization, and basic medical treatments. The centre plays a crucial role in ensuring accessible healthcare for villagers and nearby areas.

Highlights

  • Primary Healthcare: Essential medical services and basic treatments for common ailments.
  • Maternal & Child Health: ANM services, immunization, and prenatal care for mothers and children.
  • Medical Stores: Access to essential medicines and pharmaceutical products in the village.
  • Community Service: Dedicated healthcare professionals serving the local community's medical needs.
